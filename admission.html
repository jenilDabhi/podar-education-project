Our Admission Process

Online Process

  • Complete the online application form.
  • Upload the following documents:
    • Student's Birth Certificate
    • Student's Aadhaar Card
    • School Leaving Certificate
    • U-DISE Number
    • Address proof (e.g., Ration Card, Electricity Bill, Passport, Aadhaar)
    • PAN Card of any one parent/guardian
  • After submission, an email will be sent with the payment link for the first term fee if a seat is available.

Things to Remember

Admissions are on a first-come, first-served basis, and seats are limited. Admissions will only be confirmed once the necessary documents and the first term fee payment are made. Fees once paid will be strictly non-refundable.
